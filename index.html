Privacy Policy - SIAKAD

Last updated: 10 December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SIAKAD. This Privacy Policy explains how SIAKAD we collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when you use the SIAKAD mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Access

SIAKAD may request access to your device’s camera. This permission is used only for the following purposes:

  • Capturing student or staff profile photos.
  • Uploading required documents (e.g., ID card, student card).
  • Scanning QR codes for attendance or activity verification.

We do not capture photos or videos without your explicit action. Photos are only sent to our server when you choose to upload them.

2.2 Account Information

To use SIAKAD, you may be required to provide certain information, including:

  • Full name
  • Student ID / Identification Number
  • Email address
  • Relevant academic information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for academic and administrative operations, including:

  • User authentication and account management.
  • Managing academic data and administrative processes.
  • Supporting app features such as file uploads, verification, and attendance.
  • Improving and maintaining our services.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

SIAKAD does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Data may only be shared with third parties when necessary for operational purposes (such as institutional servers or hosting providers) or when required by law.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your data in accordance with applicable laws. For any data-related requests, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below.

7. Children’s Privacy

This application is intended for academic users. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years old without parental or institutional consent. If you believe data has been collected without permission, please contact us for removal.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
